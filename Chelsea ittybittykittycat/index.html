  • CAT LOAF RECIPIE: Ingredients: 300g (2 ½ cups) all-purpose flour, 170ml (¾ cup) water, 6g (2 tsp) instant yeast, 5g (2 tsp) salt, 17g (2 ½ tbsp) milk powder, 38g (3 tbsp) sugar, 28g (2 tbsp) room temperature unsalted butter, oil for the tin. Instructions: Combine dry ingredients (flour, yeast, salt, milk powder, sugar) in a stand mixer, avoiding direct contact between salt and yeast. Add water and mix, then add butter and knead until the dough is smooth and elastic. Oil the cat-shaped pan. Shape the dough into a ball and place it in the tin. Proof in a warm area for about 1 hour or until doubled in size. Remove the dough, punch it down, and reshape it, gently pressing into the ear sections of the pan. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C) and bake for 30 minutes. Let it cool in the tin for 3 minutes before transferring to a wire rack to cool completely.
