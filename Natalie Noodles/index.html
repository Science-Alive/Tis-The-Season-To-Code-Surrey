Ducky Details!

Ducks are so cute and soft, so everyone should like them! This is a website where you can learn more about ducks!

Ducky Facts:

  • Ducks are very social with other ducks
  • They have sensitive bills
  • When ducks sleep, half of their brain is sleeping and the other half is awake to be aware for predators
  • They can fly long distances
  • They communicate with different sounds ( not just quacks!)
  • More daylight encourages them to lay more eggs!

    • Duck genders:

  • Male ducks are called Drakes
  • Female ducks are called Hens
  • Baby ducks are called Ducklings!
